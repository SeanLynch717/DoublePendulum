Description

In this simulation you can play around with all the variables that effect the trajectory of a double pendulum. Drag anywhere on the screen to adjust the position of the double pendulum, and click start to watch it go! If you take a look to the sliders section, you can adjust the mass and length of the pendulum, as well as the gravity and dampening. Once you've played around with a single double pendulum, I encourage you to experiment with adding more. Have fun!
